Hi there. This is the github for Paiq BV. Perhaps you've come across some of our webapplications?

  • paiq.nl - free dutch dating website featuring matching-by-AI (blog)
  • tjetter.nl - low threshold chat 'n meet using your hyves.nl account
  • blurrypeople.com - meet strangers, or possibly your next match, using your webcam

Projects

Some of our websites have public APIs. Libraries and (example) applications that use these interfaces, made by us, are released under an open source license. They are hosted on github.

  • ilmpclient - C++ Implicit Link Comet Protocol client library.
  • dsa_verify - C library to validate a blob based on DSA public key parameters.
  • notifier - Mac and Windows client that notifies you of events on paiq.nl.
